Scout Badges

The Scout programme recognises Scouts' work and achievements in a broad range of skills with these awards.

Chief Scout's Challenge Awards For the Gold Award, the highest award a Scout can get, they must do all these challenges:
  • World
  • Skills
  • Creative
  • Outdoor
  • Adventure
  • Expedition
  • Teamwork
  • Team Leader
  • Personal
plus at least six of the activity badges:
Staged Activity Badges Stages are exactly the same all the way through Beavers, Cubs, Scouts & Explorers:
  • Nights Away
  • Hikes Away
  • Emergency Aid
  • Swimmer
  • Musician
  • Digital Citizen
  • Digital Maker
  • Community
  • Navigator
  • Air Activities
  • Water Time
  • Nautical Skills
  • Sailing
  • Paddle Sports
Scout Activity Badges Most Scouts will do some of these:
  • Communicator
  • Camper
  • My Faith
  • Athletics
  • Physical Recreation
  • Hobbies
and there are plenty more to recognise Scouts' individual hobbies: Scout Activity Badges Directory

If you are looking for something specific, try searching the text of this unofficial Scout Badge Book, which you can save for reference offline.
