Scout Badges

The Scout programme recognises Scouts' work and achievements in a broad range of skills with these awards.

Chief Scout's Challenge Awards For the Gold Award, the highest award a Scout can get, they must do all these:
  • Community
  • Fitness
  • Creative
  • Global
  • Promise
  • Outdoor
plus two of these:
  • Outdoor Plus
  • Adventure
  • Expedition
Staged Activity Badges Stages are exactly the same all the way through Beavers, Cubs, Scouts & Explorers:
  • Nights Away
  • Hikes Away
  • Emergency Aid
  • Swimmer
  • Musician
  • Information Technology
Scout Activity Badges Most Scouts will do some of these:
  • Communicator
  • Camper
  • Camp Cook
  • My Faith
  • Athlete
  • Physical Recreation
and there are plenty more to recognise Scouts' individual hobbies: Scout Activity Badges Directory
Partnership Awards Everyone in Beavers, Cubs, Scouts & Explorers can do these by working as part of a team with other communities:
  • International
  • Faith
  • Environment

If you are looking for something specific, try searching the text of this unofficial Scout Badge Book, which you can save for reading offline.
